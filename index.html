Food Database

Find nutrition facts for your favourite brands and fast-food restaurants.

Lists the nutritional content, the serving size, and the calories for a recommended serving of a food product. This helps consumers make the best decision on how much to eat, maybe when they want to eat this food, or how they can better balance their food choices throughout the day.

To find out the exact nutritional values of the food you eat as measured by number of calories or weight of its specific vitamin, mineral, fat, protein, and carbohydrate content, all you have to do is enter the name of a specific food of interest in the blank space next to the interactive button that you will click to connect directly with the remote information source. The answers will be displayed almost instantly on your screen.

Recipe Nutrition Calculator

Try Our Recipe Nutrition Calculator.

If you wish to conduct a more thorough but narrowly defined search, then our recipe nutrition calculator can help you. Filters are available to reflect any allergy restriction, diet plan, caloric limit, and specific nutrients or vitamins! All you have to do is select any filter you may need, input a meal and the database will output a list of recipes and nutritional facts that take into account any filters you may have selected.

About US
